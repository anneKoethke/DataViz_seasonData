Notes for Football Data

All data is in csv format, ready for use within standard spreadsheet applications. Please note that some abbreviations are no longer in use (in particular odds from specific bookmakers no longer used) and refer to data collected in earlier seasons. For a current list of what bookmakers are included in the dataset please visit http://www.football-data.co.uk/matches.php

Key to results data:

Date = Match Date (dd/mm/yy)
HomeTeam = Home Team
AwayTeam = Away Team
FTHG and HG = Full Time Home Team Goals (Endergebnis Heimteam)
FTAG and AG = Full Time Away Team Goals 
FTR and Res = Full Time Result (H=Home Win, D=Draw, A=Away Win)
HTHG = Half Time Home Team Goals (Halbzeit HEimteam)
HTAG = Half Time Away Team Goals
HTR = Half Time Result (H=Home Win, D=Draw, A=Away Win)

Match Statistics (where available)
Attendance = Crowd Attendance (Publikumsanzahl)
Referee = Match Referee (Name) -> wieviel Gelbe/rote K. vergibt ein bestimmter Schiri
HS = Home Team Shots (Torversuch, nicht auf's Tor) 
AS = Away Team Shots
HST = Home Team Shots on Target (Torversuche die getroffen haben oder abgewendet wurden)
AST = Away Team Shots on Target
HHW = Home Team Hit Woodwork (Pfosten-, Latentreffer)
AHW = Away Team Hit Woodwork
HC = Home Team Corners (Ecken bekommen)
AC = Away Team Corners
HF = Home Team Fouls Committed (Fouls begangen)
AF = Away Team Fouls Committed
HFKC = Home Team Free Kicks Conceded (Freisto√ü bekommen)
AFKC = Away Team Free Kicks Conceded
HO = Home Team Offsides (stand im abseits)
AO = Away Team Offsides
HY = Home Team Yellow Cards (gelb)
AY = Away Team Yellow Cards
HR = Home Team Red Cards (rot)
AR = Away Team Red Cards
